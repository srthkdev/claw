Installation

Adding the final Verifiers chatbot to your website is simple:

1

Copy the Script Tag

Copy the following script tag and paste it just before the closing </body> tag in your HTML:

<!-- Verifiers Chatbot Widget --> <script> window.chatbotConfig = { chatbotId: 30001, position: 'bottom-right', theme: 'light', baseUrl: 'http://localhost:3000', customTitle: 'Verifiers Assistant', customPlaceholder: 'Ask me about verifiers...', customWelcomeMessage: `Hello! I'm your verifiers assistant. How can I help you today?`, customButtonLabel: 'Chat with Verifiers', showBranding: true }; </script> <script src="http://localhost:3000/chatbot-widget.js" defer></script> <!-- End Verifiers Chatbot Widget -->
2

Customize Options

Modify the configuration to match your preferences:

  • position: 'bottom-right', 'bottom-left', 'top-right', or 'top-left'
  • theme: 'light' or 'dark'
  • customTitle: Chat window title
  • customPlaceholder: Input placeholder text
  • baseUrl: Your domain (e.g., 'https://yourdomain.com')
